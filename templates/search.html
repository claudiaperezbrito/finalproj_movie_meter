Gender Movie Meter

The Bechdel Test is a test that measures the representation of women in fiction. The test has 4 levels of rating:

  1. Fewer than two women in this movie.
  2. There are two or more women in this movie, but they don't talk to each other.
  3. There are two or more women in this movie, but they only talk to each other about a man.
  4. There are two or more women in this movie and they talk to each other about something other than a man.

Type in a movie title to search and see the measurement of female representation onscreen and behind the scenes.
